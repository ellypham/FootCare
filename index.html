Services

Your in-home Nursing Foot Care is safely and professionally provided in your own home (available in the Tri-Cities), by a Licensed Practical Nurse (BCLPN).

For more information, visit the Frequently Asked Questions page.

Full Foot Assessment

Each Foot Care appointment is 30 minutes to 1 hour long (depending on the treatments that need to be done), where I will do a full assessment of your feet and toes to help you understand your risk factors and identify any foot problems.

Diabetic Foot Care & Education

Individuals with Diabetes often have trouble with their feet, so a regular foot care performed by a professional is very important. I will assess your feet and treat as necessary, as well as provide you with education on how to take care of Diabetic feet day-to-day.

Thickened & Fungal Nails

Fungus thrive in warm, moist areas (like sweaty feet), so having fungal nails can happen. I will assist you in reducing nail fungus and provide you with helpful tips to treat and manage it.

Callused Feet

Calluses can occur on any part of the skin where there is repeated friction. I will help you safely reduce callus or corn on the feet, using sterilized tools.

Ingrown Toenails

Often, as we age, our ability to trim our own toenails can become hindered or difficult. I will trim and file your toenails to prevent and/or manage ingrown toenails, and fingernails on request.

Basic Foot Massage (Circulation)

At the end of your Foot Care appointment, a relaxing basic foot massage will be provided to help promote circulation.

Meet the Nurse

I am a BC Licensed Practical Nurse with certified special training in the care of feet and toes. I provide Foot Care services in the comfort of your own home, anywhere in the Tri-Cities. To ensure your safety, all instruments and tools I bring with me are fully sterilized and disinfected.

My goal is to help you achieve good foot health, improve mobility, and achieve a high quality of life.

Languages offered: English, Korean

Pricing

Each Foot Care appointment is $35, and is payable by Cash or Cheque. A receipt will be provided.
Services for Veterans are free of charge.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?

I provide Foot Care services for ingrown toenails, thickened and fungal toenails, toenail and fingernail trim, basic foot massage for circulation, and care for Diabetic patients.

What services do you not provide?

I do not provide any surgical services, prescribing of medicines or injections. In cases that require such treatments, I can make referrals to specialists and/or doctors.

How much is your service?

Each appointment is $35 CAD, payable by cash or personal cheque. A receipt will be provided.

Do you provide service to Veterans free of charge?

Yes. Service for Veterans with a valid Veteran ID is free of charge.

Do you provide a receipt?

Yes, a receipt is provided after each appointment.

How long is each appointment?

Each appointment is between 30 minutes and 1 hour, depending on the services required to be performed and the condition of the feet.

Do you do custom fit orthotics?

No, but I can make referrals to specialists that can help you with custom fit orthotics.

What license or education do you have?

I am a BC Licensed Practical Nurse, and have specialized education for Foot Care Nursing from Vancouver Community College.
